What is Biometric?

Biometric is identifying or authenticating an individual using a scan. There are different types of Biometrics although somre are visual, auitory, and behavioural. For Visual there are: Ear scan, iris recognition, retina recognition, face recognition, and fingerprint recognition. As of behavioural there is gait and typing recognition. Finally for auditory there is speaker identification.

The types of biometrics: what it does:
Ear Identifies a persons ear based on the shape of it.
Eyes-iris recognition Scans the iris to identify a person.
Eyes-retina recognition Scans the pattern of the veins in the eye.
Face recognition Scans the face for facial features.
Gait Determines a persons identity by the way how they walk.
Typing recognition Identifies a persons identity by the way a person types using unique characteristics.
Speaker identification Takes a little speech from an unkown person and then analyzes it to the known person.
